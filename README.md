## About

M&H Dynamic Tech is a South African online store specialising in **certified pre-owned laptops and phones**. Every device is checked and cleaned before it's listed, so customers know exactly what they're getting — quality tech at honest prices, delivered nationwide.

This repository holds the source for the storefront website, hosted free on GitHub Pages.

## Features

- 🛍️ **Full product catalogue** — iPhones, MacBooks, and business laptops (HP, Dell, Lenovo, Acer)
- 🔎 **Live search** — find any device instantly
- 🎚️ **Filters & sorting** — by brand, price range, and condition, with price/name sorting
- 🔀 **Grid & list views** — browse the way you prefer
- 💬 **WhatsApp checkout** — add items to the cart and send the full order straight to us on WhatsApp to confirm stock, payment, and delivery
- 📱 **Fully responsive** — works on phone, tablet, and desktop
- ⚡ **Fast & lightweight** — a single static page, no slow plugins

## How ordering works

1. The customer browses the catalogue and adds devices to their cart.
2. They tap **Checkout on WhatsApp**.
3. Their full order (items + total) is sent to **063 611 0970** as a ready-made message.
4. We confirm availability, arrange payment, and ship nationwide (2–5 working days).

> Card and Buy-Now-Pay-Later options (PayFast, Payflex, PayJustNow, Mobicred) are being added.

## Running it locally

No build tools needed — it's a plain static site.

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Open `index.html` in any web browser.

That's it.

## Deploying (GitHub Pages)

1. Upload `index.html` and the `assets` folder to this repository.
2. Go to **Settings → Pages**.
3. Under **Build and deployment**, set **Source** to *Deploy from a branch*.
4. Choose branch **main** and folder **/ (root)**, then **Save**.
5. After a minute or two, the site goes live at the link above.

## Updating products

Product details (name, price, image, description) live inside `index.html`. To add or change a device, edit its card in the product grid, and place its photo in the `assets` folder.
