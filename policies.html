↩ Returns & Refunds

Last updated: June 2026

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If something isn't right, you can return your item free of charge within 7 days of delivery, provided it's in its original condition with all accessories and packaging included.

How to return an item

  • Message us on WhatsApp (063 611 0970) with your order details and reason for return.
  • We'll confirm the return and share the steps for sending the item back.
  • Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund or replacement.

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method, usually within a few working days of us receiving and checking the returned item.

What we can't accept

  • Items returned without their original packaging and accessories.
  • Items damaged after delivery through misuse, drops, or liquid contact.
Your rights under the South African Consumer Protection Act always apply, in addition to this policy.

We deliver to addresses across South Africa using trusted couriers.

🛡️ Warranty

Last updated: June 2026

All devices sold by M&H Dynamic Tech are certified pre-owned and tested before sale. Each device carries a limited 6-month warranty covering functional faults that are not caused by the customer.

What the warranty covers

  • Functional hardware faults that develop under normal use during the warranty period.

What the warranty does not cover

  • Accidental, physical, or liquid damage.
  • Normal wear, cosmetic marks, and battery health degradation over time.
  • Faults caused by unauthorised repairs, modifications, or misuse.

Making a warranty claim

Contact us on WhatsApp with your order details and a description of the fault. Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims. Approved claims are resolved by repair, replacement, or refund at our discretion.

As these are pre-owned devices, the condition of each item is described honestly at the point of sale. If you're unsure about a device's condition, just ask us before you buy.
